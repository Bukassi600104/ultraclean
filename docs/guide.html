🔑 Section 1

Getting Access

How admin accounts are created and who can have one.

Who can access the Control Center?

Only people with an Admin account can log in to the Control Center. There is no self-registration — accounts are created manually to keep things secure. Farm managers get a separate login at a different address.

Option A — Your account already exists

Your developer pre-created your account. You either received an email with a magic link, or were given a temporary password directly. Head straight to Section 2 — Logging In.

Option B — Adding a new admin account
1

Decide who needs access

Only give admin access to people you fully trust — they can see all leads, finances, and customer data.

2

Gather the details

You'll need: their full name, email address, and a temporary password (they'll change it on first login).

3

Contact your developer

Send these details to Tony. He creates the account in the backend and assigns the Admin role. The person can then log in and immediately update their password under Settings.

⚠️

Never share your admin login. If someone else needs access, request a separate account for them. This keeps the system secure and your activity separate.

🚪 Section 2

Logging In for the First Time

How to sign in and what to do immediately after your first login.

Your Login Address
https://leads.ultratidycleaning.com/login

Bookmark this link. You will see a page titled "Control Center" with a sign-in form.

Step-by-step login
1

Enter your email

Type the email address linked to your admin account (e.g. hello@ultratidycleaning.com).

2

Enter your password

Type your password. Click the 👁 eye icon on the right side of the field to show your password as you type — click again to hide it.

3

Click "Sign In"

A spinning loader appears while it authenticates. On success, you land on your Dashboard.

❌

If you see a red error message — check your email spelling and make sure Caps Lock is off. Your password is case-sensitive.

4

Change your password immediately (first login only)

Go to Settings (click your avatar at the bottom of the sidebar) and set a strong personal password. See Section 13 for the full steps.

💡

Password requirements: At least 8 characters, one uppercase letter, and one number. Example: Bimbo2026!

🛠️ Section 15

Troubleshooting

Common issues and exactly what to do about them.

"I can't log in — it says invalid credentials"

  • Double-check your email — no typos, no extra spaces
  • Make sure Caps Lock is off
  • Click the eye icon to see your password as you type it
  • If still locked out → contact your developer to reset the password

"The page is loading slowly"

  • Check your internet connection
  • Press F5 (or Ctrl+R) to refresh the page
  • The dashboard may take 1-2 seconds after a period of inactivity — this is normal

"I deleted something by accident"

🚨

Deleted leads, posts, and managers cannot be recovered from the dashboard. Contact your developer immediately — data can be recovered directly from the database if caught early.

"A farm manager says they can't log in"

1

Go to Farm Managers in the sidebar

2

Find their account — check for a red "Suspended" badge

If suspended → click Reactivate.

3

If not suspended → click Reset Password

Set a new password and share it with them via WhatsApp.

4

Tell them to log in at farm.primefieldagric.com

"The DBA registration price is showing wrong"

Go to Courses → Edit and update the price to match exactly what is set in your Stripe dashboard. Both must show the same amount.

"New leads are not appearing"

  • Check your internet connection
  • Refresh the Leads page — a page refresh always pulls the latest data
  • The dashboard updates in real time, but connectivity issues can delay it

"I want to give someone else admin access"

Contact your developer to create a new admin account. Do not share your own login — each person should have their own account.
